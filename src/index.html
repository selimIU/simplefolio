Borg El Arab

A villa was created as a residence for a family who enjoys spending the majority of their time at home. The family includes a father who is an engineer, a mother who stays at home with their three daughters, and a son who is a creative engineer. The parents prefer neoclassical style, while the son prefers modern, minimal, and contemporary design, so we tried to merge between modren minimal style and neoclassic.

Mediterranean lighten gate

Alexandria is known for its lighthouse, which is one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World and could lead ships and boats up-to of 50 km away. I used the lighthouse concept for my museum design, the philosophy is, to lead anyone who wants to know about Alexandria and its history. As a result, the museum serves as both a gateway to the past and the eastern gateway to Alexandria.
